Built around two jobs creators actually do

Independent music creators have two repetitive problems: figuring out which songs and audiences are working, and getting new releases out on a steady cadence. Music System covers both.

Per-song performance dashboard

See views, likes, comments, shares, and follower growth for every song you publish. Sort by best-performing, weakest, fastest-rising. Compare release weeks side by side.

Audience signal, not vanity metrics

Music System surfaces leading indicators — completion rate, save rate, week-over-week velocity — so you understand which song actually resonated, not just which one happened to trend.

Release scheduling for TikTok

Plan your release calendar in one view. Queue up videos for upload as drafts on your authorized TikTok account, then publish on the cadence you set.

Cross-release learning

The product looks across your full catalog and tells you what your audience reliably responds to: tempo, mood, length, hook placement.

How Music System works

Each creator who joins the beta connects their own TikTok account through the standard TikTok Login Kit OAuth flow. Music System never touches accounts you don't authorize.

  1. Connect your TikTok

    Sign in with TikTok. We use Login Kit and request only the scopes needed for analytics and scheduling.

  2. We pull your public metrics

    Music System reads your published video list and engagement stats so the dashboard reflects your real performance.

  3. You schedule releases

    Drop in a video file, set a caption and hashtags, pick a publish time. Music System uploads it to your TikTok via the Content Posting API.

  4. Iterate from data

    Each new release gets compared against your catalog — so the next one is informed, not guessed.

Who Music System is for

Music System is built for independent music creators publishing original songs to their own TikTok account. It is not a media-management agency tool, not a posting tool for brand accounts, and not a way to manage someone else's content.

Compliance & trust

Music System integrates with TikTok through TikTok's official Login Kit and Content Posting API. We follow TikTok's Developer Terms of Service and only request the scopes our features require.

Standards we follow

OAuth 2.0 with PKCE for desktop login. Scopes requested only when needed. Tokens stored encrypted at rest, scoped to each creator.

What we never do

Music System never posts to a TikTok account we are not explicitly authorized for. We do not scrape, we do not impersonate, and we do not publish anyone else's content.

Disconnect any time

A creator can revoke Music System's access from their TikTok account settings. Once revoked, we stop pulling metrics and clear stored tokens.
