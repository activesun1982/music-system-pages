Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-01

This Privacy Policy explains what information Music System (“Music System”, “we”, “us”) collects when you use the product, how we use that information, who we share it with, and the choices you have over your data. Music System is an analytics and release-scheduling product for independent music creators.

1. What information we collect

Music System collects only the information needed to provide the analytics and scheduling features you request.

1.1 Information you provide directly

  • Beta access requests: if you submit your email through the beta access form on our homepage, we collect that email solely to contact you about beta availability.
  • Content you upload: if you use the scheduling feature, you provide a video file, caption, hashtags, and a publish time.

1.2 Information collected through TikTok’s API

When you authorize Music System through TikTok’s Login Kit, the data we receive depends on the scopes you approve at the consent screen. The table below describes each scope and the data it provides.

ScopeData Music System receivesWhy
user.info.basicYour TikTok open_id and a stable identifierTo identify the authenticated creator after OAuth.
user.info.profileDisplay name, avatar URL, profile link, bio, verified statusTo label the dashboard correctly for the connected creator.
user.info.statsAggregate stats: follower count, following count, likes count, video countTo show top-line dashboard tiles.
video.listList of your public videos and engagement metrics (views, likes, comments, shares)To power per-song performance views and cross-release comparison.
video.uploadRequired for uploading a video file to your TikTok account as a draftTo support the scheduling feature.
video.publishRequired for publishing uploaded content to your TikTok profileTo support direct-publish scheduling.

1.3 Information collected automatically

When you visit the Music System website, our hosting provider may log standard request metadata (IP address, user agent, timestamp). This information is used for security and basic site availability, not for tracking or advertising. The website does not use third-party tracking, advertising, or analytics scripts.

2. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the analytics dashboard and scheduling features you have authorized.
  • Authenticate your TikTok account and maintain your session.
  • Respond to your beta access request or support questions.
  • Maintain the security and reliability of the service.
  • Comply with applicable law and TikTok’s Developer Terms of Service.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising or for training third-party models. We do not use Music System to access TikTok accounts other than the one you have authorized.

3. How we share information

We share information only as needed to operate the product and only with the categories of recipients listed below.

  • TikTok: when you connect your account, we exchange tokens and content with TikTok’s API as required to deliver the scheduling and analytics features.
  • Infrastructure providers: we use cloud and hosting providers to run Music System; these providers process data on our behalf under standard data processing terms.
  • Legal authorities: we may disclose information if required by valid legal process or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Music System, our creators, or the public.

4. Data retention

We retain TikTok access and refresh tokens for as long as you remain connected to Music System, and we retain analytics data we have fetched on your behalf so the dashboard can show historical trends. If you revoke Music System’s access from your TikTok account settings, or you ask us to delete your data, we will delete your tokens and your stored analytics within a reasonable period (typically within 30 days), subject to legal retention obligations.

5. Your choices and rights

  • Disconnect at any time: revoke Music System’s access from your TikTok account settings. Once revoked, Music System immediately stops accessing your TikTok data.
  • Access, correction, deletion: contact us to request a copy of the data we hold about you, to correct it, or to delete it.
  • Beta email: reply “unsubscribe” to any message we send and we will remove your email from the beta list.

6. Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect data, including TLS in transit and encryption of OAuth tokens at rest. No system is perfectly secure. If we become aware of a breach affecting your information, we will notify affected creators and applicable authorities as required by law.

7. International transfers

Music System operates globally and your information may be processed in countries other than the one you reside in. Where required, we rely on lawful transfer mechanisms (such as standard contractual clauses) for cross-border data transfers.

8. Children’s privacy

Music System is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of the page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be highlighted on the homepage or communicated to connected creators.

10. Contact

Privacy questions, data access requests, or deletion requests can be sent to activesunandrew@gmail.com.
